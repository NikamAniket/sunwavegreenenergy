Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers to help you understand solar systems and claim your subsidies.

The cost depends on your required capacity. For instance, our premium 3 KW solar setup starts at a special rate of ₹1,99,999. In addition, residential systems qualify for central government subsidies which can reduce your direct investment cost significantly.

Under the PM Surya Ghar scheme, the government offers standard subsidies for residential rooftop solar systems. We handle all paperwork, applications, and compliance filings for you to make claiming your subsidy smooth and direct.

We provide a comprehensive 25-Year Performance Warranty on the solar PV modules. Inverters and custom structure components are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties, and our installation works are fully backed by our local service support team.

For a standard residential solar installation (typically between 3 KW to 10 KW), on-site physical setup takes between 3 to 5 business days. Net-metering approval and grid connection timing depend on local discom approvals, which we coordinate for you.

An On Grid solar system is connected directly to your local utility power grid. It feeds excess solar power generated during the day back into the grid via net-metering. Your utility credit balances your grid usage, drastically dropping your electricity bill. This is the most popular and affordable type of system.

A Hybrid solar system is connected to both the utility grid and a custom solar battery bank. This allows you to generate solar power, back up essential household loads during power cuts using batteries, and export any excess generation back to the utility grid.

Yes, absolutely! By producing your own solar power on your rooftop, you draw less power from the utility company. Through Net Metering, any surplus electricity you generate is sent to the grid, giving you credits that offset your bills by up to 90%.
